Concepto

LittleRegress is a project created to express free age regression within Minecraft.

This is a highly customizable open-source project designed so that users can add their own custom content. The datapack is under the GNU General Public License v3.0, which means that any creation based on LittleRegress must also be open-source and share the same license.

The main branch of the project aims not to include inappropriate content or anything that compromises the integrity of users. If any issue arises with someone or with specific content, the implementation of that content will be reconsidered. If a user wishes to add custom content, they are welcome to reach out through the various communication channels provided by the project, and through those, propose ideas or collaborate to implement features in development. If the content the user wishes to add goes against the datapack’s policies, their creation will not be denied; instead, they are completely free to fork the project and work on their version to include their implementation. However, we do not take responsibility for any problems that may arise from that version of the project. This is to separate inappropriate content from the main version, while also respecting every user’s freedom of expression.

Content Regulations

Due to its philosophy of providing a safe exploration environment, the datapack follows these development rules:

  1. • No content including nudity or sexual suggestions will be added.
  2. • Although the datapack includes elements like diapers, which could be seen as a fetish by some, its purpose is not to portray these as sexual in nature. The use of such elements is left to the discretion of the user.
  3. • If any user encounters issues with non-toggleable content, a discussion will take place regarding its re-implementation, and efforts will be made to make that content optional.
  4. • If a mechanic is added that may be unpleasant for users who are more sensitive to certain content, it will be optional by default and can be enabled or disabled in the datapack settings. However, please note that this may not apply to older versions of the datapack.
  5. • The LittleRegress team is not always obligated to comply with every demand or complaint from the community.

Regulaciones de contenido

El datapack debido a su filosofia de proporcionar un entorno seguro de exploración se atiene a las siguientes reglas para su desarrollo:

  1. • No se agregará contenido que incluyá desnudos o incitaciones sexuales.
  2. • Aunque el datapack incluya contenido como pañales que pueden ser vistos como un fetiche, su objectivo no es representar esos elementos como un complemento sexual, por lo que queda a disposición del usuario como quiere utilizar esos elementos.
  3. • Cuando un usuario del datapack presente algún problema con contenido no desactivable, se llevará a cabo un dialogo sobre su re-implementación y se procurará hacer que ese contenido sea opcional.
  4. • Cuando se añada una mecanica que pueda ser repulsiva para algunos usuarios con mayor sensibilidad al contenido, por defecto esta sera una mecanica opcional y podrá ser activada o desactivada en las configuraciones del datapack, sin embargo, hay que tener en cuenta que esto puede no aplicarse en versiones viejas del datapack.
  5. • El equipo de LittleRegress no siempre tendrá la obligación de cumplir todo lo que su comunidad o las quejas exijan.

Proposito

LittleRegress is a symbolic expression and self-exploration, a symbolic safe environment that takes advantage of the freedom Minecraft provides.

LittleRegress is not a therapy, it's a place where people can play, feel small, imagine being cared for, or explore their softer and more vulnerable side — all within a video game that allows for creativity without putting anyone at physical risk.

One of the core ideas of LittleRegress is to encourage conscious or symbolic regression, which involves helping the user reach a state of vulnerability and safety as a way to ease stress or anxiety-related issues. It’s important to clarify that our team does not include any psychologists, so even though this type of experience may be self-induced, it should ideally be done under the supervision of a responsible person, and if any issues arise, you should seek psychological support at a health center as soon as possible.

This datapack is meant to be a symbolic and expressive tool within Minecraft. It is not guided therapy and does not replace professional psychological care. If you choose to explore your inner child or vulnerable side, make sure you are in a physically and emotionally safe space, and preferably with someone who can support you if needed. It’s okay to feel small — but you are also valuable just as you are now.
