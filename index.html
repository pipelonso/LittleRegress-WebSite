LITTLEREGRESS

LittleRegress en un datapack para minecraft que añade el rol de bebé y cuidador a tu experiencia de minecraft, teniendo en cuenta la seguridad del usuario en un entorno de un solo jugador y multijugador, explorando experiencias aptas para la terapia de regresion, uso por la comunidad ABDL y todas sus variantes, ademas de ser de codigo abierto y totalmente modificable por el usuario.

El datapack actualmente usar elementos creados como lo son los pañales, ropa, cuna, cambiador, bañera, inodoro, chupon de bebe, silla de bebe y demas.

El datapack se encuentra en desarrollo por lo que el contenido puede cambiar.

LittleRegress is a datapack for Minecraft that adds the baby and caregiver roles to your Minecraft experience, taking into account user safety in both single-player and multiplayer environments. It explores experiences suitable for regression therapy, catering to the ABDL (Adult Baby Diaper Lover) community and all its variants. Additionally, it is open-source and fully customizable by the user.

The current datapack provides the opportunity use items specifically created such as diapers, clothing, crib, changing table, bathtub, toilet, baby pacifier, baby chair, and more.

Please note that the datapack is still in development, so the content may undergo changes.

1.20.4

Due to compatibility issues with the latest versions of Minecraft from 1.20.5 onwards, we have decided to restart the development of the datapack. This decision was not made lightly and every effort was made to update the datapack to the latest versions, which currently range from 1.20.5 to 1.21. However, these attempts were not successful and have caused many stability issues.

The current version of the datapack will not be deleted, and bugs will be fixed as much as possible if they are reported to the email abthinksdl@gmail.com. I will be attentive to any suggestions from any user as ideas to implement in the datapack.

It is likely that the development will take longer than expected. Please keep in mind that I am the only programmer and designer on my team, although there are people who help me by testing the content and finding errors.

The second version of the datapack will have new content, improved dimensions, structures, more clothing, more mechanics, much more content, and better balance.

If you want to contribute to the development of the datapack on a completely voluntary basis, I will be more than happy to receive help in the development.

If you have good knowledge of mcfunction for Minecraft Java from 1.20.5 onwards and want to help, you are welcome. The project will always be open-source, so you can contribute to the project directly.

Note: This datapack will never be sold. The content created by and for this datapack aims to be free and give any Minecraft user the opportunity to experience the content that it provides.
